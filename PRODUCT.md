# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Freelancers and instructors who track unpaid work and hold managers accountable. They use the app both at a desk (reviewing balances, generating invoices) and on the go (logging sessions between commitments). They are stressed about money they are owed and want clarity, not decoration.

## Product Purpose

A multi-user SaaS that logs work sessions, calculates earnings, records payments, and generates formal documents (invoices, demand letters). Success is when a user can see exactly what they are owed, down to the cent, in under five seconds, and feel confident enough to act on that number.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, direct, empowering. The interface takes your side. No hedging, no softening. Numbers are the authority. The tool disappears into the task.

## Anti-references

- Overly playful or cute interfaces: no illustrations, no emoji, no bright pastels, no confetti.
- Heavy decorative dashboards with hero metrics, gradient cards, and cookie-cutter chart layouts.
- Generic SaaS cream-and-navy templates that could be selling anything.

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity above decoration.** Every pixel earns its place. If it does not help the user understand their money, it does not belong.
2. **Numbers are the interface.** Balances, totals, and dates are the primary visual elements. Typography and weight carry the hierarchy, not color or cards.
3. **Earned familiarity.** Follow established tool conventions. No invented affordances. If Linear and Raycast users feel at home, we have succeeded.
4. **Dark-native, light-capable.** The dark theme is not an afterthought. Both themes must feel equally intentional and complete.
5. **Restraint lets the data speak.** One accent color, tinted neutrals. The variance comes from the numbers, not the chrome.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AAA target. Higher contrast ratios than standard AA across all themes. All interactive elements must have visible focus indicators. Reduced-motion support required. No information conveyed by color alone.
